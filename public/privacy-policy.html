Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Version 1.0

1. Overview

MS Hospital Diversion ("the App") is operated by Life Still Goes On Publishing, founded by Bryan "BW" White, Pearl, Mississippi. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects information when used by authorized emergency medical services (EMS) personnel, hospital charge nurses, and EMS supervisors in Mississippi.

This App is intended exclusively for credentialed public safety professionals. It is not a consumer application and is not directed at the general public or individuals under the age of 18.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Work email address (provided by your agency or hospital)
  • Job role (EMS Crew, Charge Nurse, or Supervisor/Admin)
  • Unit ID or Hospital affiliation
  • Geographic region assignment

Operational Data

  • Hospital diversion status updates you publish (status, reason, estimated clear time)
  • Timestamp and user identity attached to each status update for audit purposes

Device & Technical Data

  • Device GPS coordinates — only when you tap "Get My Location" on the Nearest Hospitals screen. Location is never collected passively or in the background without your action.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token — used only to deliver push notifications about diversion status changes
  • App version and basic crash/error logs (anonymous)
We do not collect: patient information, protected health information (PHI), personal financial data, social security numbers, or any data unrelated to EMS operational status.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and enforce role-based access
  • To display real-time hospital diversion status to authorized EMS personnel
  • To send push notifications when hospital statuses change to DIVERSION or BYPASS
  • To maintain an audit log of status updates for operational accountability
  • To calculate nearest-hospital distance using your voluntarily shared location
  • To improve app performance and fix technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers or third-party data brokers.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore Database and Firebase Authentication), which is hosted on Google Cloud Platform servers located in the United States. Firebase maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance and industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.

Access to hospital status data is restricted by Firestore Security Rules that enforce role-based permissions. Only authenticated users with verified roles may read or write operational data.

Your account is provisioned by your agency administrator. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

5. Location Data

The App requests access to your device location only when you explicitly tap "Get My Location" on the Nearest Hospitals screen. This is used solely to calculate distances to Mississippi hospitals and estimate drive times. Location coordinates are:

  • Processed on-device for distance calculation
  • Never stored on our servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never tracked passively or in the background

You may deny location permission at any time without affecting other app functionality.

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, the App sends push notifications when Mississippi hospitals change their diversion status to DIVERSION or BYPASS. These notifications are operational alerts — not marketing. You may disable notifications at any time in your device Settings.

FCM device tokens are stored in your user profile solely to deliver these notifications and are automatically refreshed or removed as needed.

7. Data Retention

  • Active account data — retained while your account is active
  • Status update audit logs — retained for 90 days for operational accountability
  • Location data — never stored; processed on-device only
  • Inactive accounts — deactivated by your agency administrator; data removed within 30 days of deactivation request

8. HIPAA Notice

MS Hospital Diversion handles operational diversion status data only — not patient records, treatment information, or any Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA. Diversion status (e.g., "ED at capacity") is operational information, not patient data.

Users must not enter patient-identifiable information into this application.

9. Your Rights

You may request at any time:

  • Access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate account information
  • Deletion of your account and associated data
  • Removal of your FCM notification token

Direct requests to your agency administrator or to privacy@lifestillgoeson.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, push notifications (Firebase Privacy)
  • Google Cloud Platform — Cloud Functions, hosting (GCP Privacy)

No advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or social media plugins are included in this application.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be communicated through the App or via email to registered users. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
