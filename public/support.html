Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a real login account?
Contact your agency EMS director or hospital ED manager. Accounts are provisioned by your organization's designated administrator. Email support@lifestillgoeson.com if you need help identifying your admin.
How do I update my hospital's diversion status?
Log in as a Charge Nurse, tap your facility on the Status Board or the "My Facility" tab, then tap "Update Diversion Status." Select the new status, reason, and estimated clear time, then tap Publish. The update is live within seconds across all EMS devices statewide.
How do EMS crews use the Nearest Hospitals screen?
Tap the "Nearest" tab (compass icon), then tap "Get My Location." The app uses your GPS to rank all 24 Mississippi hospitals by distance and estimated drive time, filtered by acceptance status.
Why is a hospital showing the wrong status?
Status updates are published by hospital charge nurses or EMS supervisors. If a status appears stale or incorrect, contact the hospital's ED directly using the phone number on the Hospital Detail screen. You can also tap "EMS Status Update" to submit a field correction.
I'm not receiving push notifications — how do I fix this?
On iPhone: Settings → MS Diversion → Notifications → Allow Notifications. On Android: Settings → Apps → MS Diversion → Notifications → Enable. Also ensure the app is not in Low Power Mode, which can delay notifications.
How do I add hospitals to my Favorites list?
Tap the ★ star icon on any hospital card or on the Hospital Detail screen. Saved hospitals appear in your Settings → Saved Hospitals list. Tap "★ FAV" filter on the Status Board to show favorites only.
What does BYPASS mean vs DIVERSION?
ADVISORY — hospital is approaching capacity; consider alternatives. DIVERSION — hospital is requesting units be rerouted; use nearest alternative unless critical. BYPASS — hospital is closed to EMS transport; do not transport except life threat with no alternative.
Is patient data ever transmitted through this app?
No. This app transmits operational diversion status only — not patient records, PHI, or any individually identifiable health information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
The app isn't loading — what should I do?
Check your internet connection. Close and reopen the app. If the problem persists, log out and log back in. If issues continue after 5 minutes, contact support below — there may be a server maintenance window.

Role Guide

  • EMS Crew — Read-only access to all hospital statuses statewide. Can view map, nearest hospitals, alerts feed, and hospital detail. Can submit field status observations.
  • Charge Nurse — Full read access plus ability to update your assigned hospital's diversion status, reason, and estimated clear time.
  • Supervisor / Admin — Full read/write access to all hospitals. Access to admin dashboard with system-wide overview and region breakdown. Can manage user accounts.
