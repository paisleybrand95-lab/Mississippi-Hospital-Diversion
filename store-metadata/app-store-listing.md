## DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max — use for both iOS & Android)

MS Hospital Diversion is the official real-time diversion status tool for Mississippi EMS personnel, charge nurses, and emergency management supervisors.

Built for the field. Designed for speed.

WHAT IT DOES
Instantly shows the diversion status of all 24 major Mississippi hospitals — from UMMC Level I Trauma in Jackson to rural Level IV facilities — updated in real time by hospital staff and EMS units on the ground.

FOR EMS CREWS
• See which hospitals are OPEN, on ADVISORY, on DIVERSION, or BYPASS
• Get GPS-based nearest hospital list with estimated drive times
• View diversion reasons (capacity, staffing, trauma bay full, MCI, and more)
• See estimated clear times so you can plan transport
• Tap any hospital to call the ED direct line instantly
• Save favorite hospitals for quick access
• Receive instant alerts when a hospital changes status

FOR CHARGE NURSES
• Log in to your facility and publish status updates directly from the app
• Set diversion reason, estimated clear time, and status level in seconds
• View your facility's full update history for the shift

FOR EMS SUPERVISORS & ADMINS
• System-wide dashboard showing all 24 hospitals at a glance
• Region breakdown — Central, North, South, Coast, East, Delta, Southwest
• Active diversion list with reasons and estimated clear times
• Live alert feed of every status change across the state

HOSPITALS COVERED
All major Mississippi health systems including:
UMMC · Baptist Medical · St. Dominic · Forrest General · NMMC Tupelo
Memorial Gulfport · Singing River · Anderson Regional · Delta Regional
And 15 more facilities across all 7 regions of the state

BUILT FOR PUBLIC SAFETY
Developed by Bryan "BW" White — public safety administrator, EMT, law enforcement officer, and U.S. Navy Hospital Corpsman veteran with nearly four decades of service.
