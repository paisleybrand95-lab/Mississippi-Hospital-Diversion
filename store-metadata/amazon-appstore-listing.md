## LONG DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max — Amazon "Long Description" field)

MS Hospital Diversion is the real-time diversion status network built
exclusively for Mississippi emergency medical services personnel.

Designed for three roles — EMS crews in the field, charge nurses at the
hospital, and EMS supervisors — the app keeps every authorized user
synchronized on diversion status across all 24 major Mississippi hospitals,
from UMMC Level I Trauma in Jackson to rural Level IV facilities.

─── FOR EMS CREWS ───────────────────────────────────────────────

Know before you roll. The live Status Board shows every Mississippi
hospital sorted by severity the moment you open the app.

• OPEN — accepting all transports
• ADVISORY — nearing capacity, consider alternatives
• DIVERSION — requesting reroute; use nearest alternative
• BYPASS — closed to EMS transport; do not transport

GPS Nearest Hospitals uses your Fire tablet or phone's location to rank
all 24 facilities by distance and estimated drive time — filtered by
which hospitals are currently accepting patients. Critical for field
crews transporting unstable patients.

Push notifications alert your unit the moment any hospital escalates
to DIVERSION or BYPASS. No more radio tag — the update finds you.

Tap any hospital to call the ED direct line instantly from the app.

─── FOR CHARGE NURSES ───────────────────────────────────────────

Update your hospital's diversion status in seconds. Log in, tap your
facility, choose the status, reason, and estimated clear time — and
the update is live statewide within seconds across every EMS device
in Mississippi.

Status reasons include: ED Overcrowding, Capacity Overload, Staffing
Shortage, Trauma Bay Full, ICU at Capacity, NICU at Capacity,
Psychiatric Hold Full, Equipment Failure, Isolation Protocols, Mass
Casualty Incident, and more.

─── FOR EMS SUPERVISORS & ADMINS ────────────────────────────────

The Admin Dashboard gives supervisors a system-wide view: total
hospitals by status, region breakdown across all seven Mississippi
regions (Central, North, South, Coast, East, Delta, Southwest), active
diversion list with reasons and estimated clear times, and a real-time
alert feed of every status change statewide.
